Statistics Lab

Welcome to this applet.

Here, you can explore the Standard Normal Distribution, the Sampling Distribution of X̄, and Confidence Intervals.

By adjusting the parameters, you can see how distributions and intervals change, helping you develop a clear understanding of key statistical concepts.

We encourage you to experiment and reflect on your observations. Office hours are available if you want to discuss your observations or clarify concepts.
